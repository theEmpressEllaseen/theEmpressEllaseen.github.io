about us:

The factory was built and put into operation with a design capacity of 110 tons of processed milk per shift in August 1977. The construction of a new plant in the Bryansk region was caused by the need to process "extra" milk, the introduction of waste-free technologies.

At the stage of its formation, the company mainly produced weight products, which accounted for 70% of the total output. Drinking milk, sour cream, cottage cheese, curd mass - were packaged in flasks, butter-in cardboard boxes. Only kefir, some drinking milk, and a small amount of sour cream were bottled in glass jars.

The main part of the accepted milk - 300-350 tons-was separated for the production of butter and skimmed milk powder. The capacity of the SOM shop was 140 tons of processed feed per day. Within a few years, the volume of processing increased to 450-500 tons per day.
